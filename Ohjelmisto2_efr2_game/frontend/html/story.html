Story

You are an American that were on trip around the Russia in 1994 (right after Berlin's wall fall),
but when you were on your way to the airport, you realized that you lost your passport at some cafe,
but you don't remember in which city. You don't have any money left, but you still have your Coca-Cola,
that are valued by Russian citizens.

Will you find your passport and fly back home or will you get stuck in Russia forever?

✕

Manual

Your current station is marked as a yellow marker.
* You can only move to neighbor cities.
* Press the red marker to see a popup window with buttons.
* You can select the green train, which costs more (2 Coca-Cola), or Diesel (1 Coca-Cola).
* When you move, you will encounter different events that affect your balance of Coca-Cola.
* One movement costs 1 Coca-Cola.
* Move between stations to find the passport.
* If your Coca-Cola balance reaches 0, it's Game Over.
