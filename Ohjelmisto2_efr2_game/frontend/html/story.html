Story

You are an American that were on trip around the Russia in 1994 (right after Berlin's wall fall),

but when you were on your way to the airport, you realized that you lost your passport at some cafe

but you don't remember in which city. You don't have any money left, but you still have your Coca-Cola

that are valued by Russian citizens. Will you find your passport and fly back home or will you get stuck

in Russia forever?

Manual

You need to find your passport, which is hidden in a random city.

When you arrive at stations, you can only move between neighboring cities.

As you play, at some point you would be able to memorize first letter of the city by consuming PRIME.

One movement costs one PRIME. You can either lose or get Coca-Cola at random events.
